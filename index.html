Frequently Asked Questions

What is an invisible character?

An invisible character, also known as an empty character, is a Unicode character that takes up space but doesn't have a visible glyph. We primarily use U+2800 (Braille Pattern Blank). This allows for a blank copy and paste that looks empty but is recognized by most systems as a valid character.

How do I copy the blank text?

It's simple! Just click the "Copy" button. The invisible text will be copied to your clipboard, ready to be pasted. This is the easiest way to copy blank text.

Is this different from a regular space?

Yes, completely. Many websites and apps trim or collapse regular spaces. The blank space copy character from our tool is a unique Unicode character that isn't treated like a regular space, allowing you to use it for special formatting.

Why do I need a blank text generator?

Finding and typing these special Unicode characters is difficult. A Blank text generator provides a one-click solution to get the exact empty text you need, whether it's for a username, a message, or a post.
